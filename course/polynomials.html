    Key Points

    1. KEY #1

      poly equations are equations that form a straight line when graphed. Here are some things to keep in mind:

      • Variables, constants, and coefficients are parts of a poly equation.
      • What you do on one side do to the other side of the equals sign.
      • Combine like-terms

    2. KEY #2

      FOIL stands for first, outer, inner, then last. This means:

      • Multiply the FIRST term in each binomial.
      • Multiply the OUTER terms in each binomial.
      • Multiply the INNER terms in each binomial.
      • Multiply the LAST terms in each binomial.

    3. KEY #3

      Substitution means replacing a variable with a number or another expression.
